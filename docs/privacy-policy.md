# Privacy Policy — AI Browser CoPilot

**Last updated:** 2026-03-28

## Data We Collect
AI Browser CoPilot processes browser data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your browsing data to external servers.

## Local Data Only
- Page content, screenshots, and form data are processed entirely on your device
- Activity logs are stored locally in your browser's extension storage
- Tool permissions and settings are stored locally

## Setup Telemetry (Optional)
During initial setup, we may collect anonymized setup completion events (e.g., "setup started", "setup completed") to improve the onboarding experience. This telemetry:
- Contains no page content or browsing data
- Is only collected with your explicit consent
- Can be opted out at any time

## Email Collection
If you provide your email during setup troubleshooting, it is used solely to provide setup assistance and is not shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
- **Payment Provider**: If you upgrade to Pro, payment is handled by our payment provider. We do not store credit card information.
- **Chrome Web Store**: Extension updates are distributed through the Chrome Web Store.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact us via the "Report a Problem" link in the extension.
